About BrewHaus Bar

Born in 2021, BrewHaus Bar is crafted for great conversations, easy music and consistently good food. Located in HRBR Layout 1st Block, Kalyan Nagar, we bring together local warmth and a lively, contemporary bar experience.

Neighbourhood Spirit

We’re a community-first bar with generous seating across multiple zones so large groups and families have room to relax. Expect swift service, friendly faces and a playlist that never shouts over your chat.
